Weekly · Monthly · Seasonal context

New listings

Fresh inventory is trickling in week-over-week. Well-priced, move-in-ready homes are still drawing multiple offers within the first weekend.

Pending sales

Pendings remain brisk for turnkey properties; buyers are acting quickly on homes priced to the comps.

Days on market

Median days on market stays low for desirable homes, while overpriced listings linger and see reductions.

Median price

Month-over-month prices are holding firm across San Jose, Santa Clara, Sunnyvale and Mountain View, supported by limited supply.

Inventory

Active listings are gradually building from winter lows, giving buyers slightly more choice heading into summer.

Sale-to-list ratio

Homes continue to close at or above asking in competitive segments, especially single-family homes near top schools and transit.

Spring surge

Spring is historically the most active season — more listings and more buyers. Sellers often capture premium pricing and faster sales.

Summer steady

Activity stays healthy through summer, with families aiming to close before the school year.

Fall & winter

Inventory thins out, but motivated buyers remain. Less competition can mean opportunity for well-prepared buyers.

Know the Market

Factors that affect buying & selling in the South Bay

The Silicon Valley market has its own drivers. These are the forces I watch most closely for my clients.

Tech job market

Hiring, layoffs, RSU vesting and IPO activity at major employers directly shape buyer demand and budgets across the South Bay.

Mortgage rates

Every move in the 30-year fixed shifts monthly payments and purchasing power — critical in a high-price market.

Inventory & lock-in

Homeowners holding low pandemic-era mortgages are slow to sell, keeping supply tight and supporting prices.

Schools & location

Top-rated school boundaries, commute access and walkability command persistent premiums.

Taxes & Prop 13

Property tax basis, transfer taxes and capital-gains considerations factor heavily into seller timing.

Seasonality

Spring and early summer bring the most activity; timing your listing can affect both speed and price.
